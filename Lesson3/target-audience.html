Target Audience

The purpose of Stellar Weather is to target audience who are interested in current or future weather condition. Audience will be able to use the information to plan for outdoor activities for specific day and to prepare accordingly.

Scenario 1

Gabriela would like to have a hiking adventure for about 3 days with her family and some friends. She is going to explore one of the national parks and would like to know rain forecast for next week.

Scenario 2

Andres has been training for one of the best soccer competitions for two weeks. He would like to check for the weather for the next week for the last trainings.

Scenario 3

Gabriela is going to have a retreat with her employees.They are going to spend a week in a cabin at the mountains, she would like to know the best outfits she can wear depending on the weather.

Scenario 4

Andres would like to help his team with some extra training for the final cup. He would like to know when is going to be the perfect sunny day to plan the training session.
